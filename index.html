What is Criptio?

Criptio is an opensource interactive Command Line Tool based on Node.js for encrypt and decrypt any kind of files. You can install with npm packager npm install criptio -g. It's quite simple, take a look.

Nowdays privacy is important. Mozilla in the article 7 Encryption and Privacy Tips (which I recommend reading) talks about privacy tips, and the first tip says

Take steps to encrypt your communications. Encryption is the encoding of data so that only people with a special key can unlock it. Most modern communications systems use strong encryption. iMessage, Signal, and WhatsApp all provide encrypted text messaging. Facetime, Signal, Firefox Hello, and Google Hangouts all provide encrypted video conferencing. E-mail, ordinary phone calls, and SMS do not provide strong security.

This is so true, we need to take care about our privacy, even though we take privacy embedded in our modern tools, this is not always true. I am so sure it has happend to you to send an email that contains private data (images, text document etc), have you ever thought about if someone gets a temporary access to your email account? Yes, can access and view your private documents in clear.
Criptio is here help you to fix this, in the simplest way possible.
